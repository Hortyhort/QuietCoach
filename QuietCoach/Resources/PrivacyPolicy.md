# Quiet Coach Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Overview

Quiet Coach is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe your practice sessions are personal, and we've built the app to keep them that way.

## Data Collection

### Audio Recordings

- **What we collect:** Audio recordings you create during practice sessions
- **How it's used:** Analyzed on your device to provide feedback on clarity, pacing, tone, and confidence
- **Where it's stored:** Only on your device, in the app's private storage
- **Sharing:** Never uploaded, transmitted, or shared with anyone
- **Retention:** Deleted when you delete a session or uninstall the app

### Speech Recognition

- **Technology:** Apple's on-device Speech framework
- **Opt-in, default off:** You must enable transcription in Settings
- **Processing:** All transcription happens locally on your device
- **Fallback:** When transcription is off, coaching uses audio-only metrics and no transcript is stored
- **Data sent to Apple:** None â€” we use on-device recognition only

### Analytics (Optional)

If you opt in to analytics in Settings:

- **What we collect:** Anonymous usage patterns (e.g., which scenarios are popular, session completion rates)
- **What we don't collect:** Audio content, transcriptions, personal identifiers
- **Purpose:** Improve app features and user experience
- **You can opt out:** Settings > Privacy > Analytics

### Crash Reporting (Optional)

If you opt in to crash reporting:

- **What we collect:** Technical information about app crashes
- **What we don't collect:** Audio, personal data, or session content
- **Purpose:** Fix bugs and improve stability
- **You can opt out:** Settings > Privacy > Crash Reporting

## Data We Never Collect

- Your name, email, or contact information (no account required)
- Location data
- Device identifiers for tracking
- Content of your speech or transcriptions off-device
- Any data for advertising purposes

## Third-Party Services

### Apple Services
- **Speech Recognition:** On-device only, no data sent to Apple
- **App Store:** Purchase data handled by Apple per their privacy policy
- **StoreKit:** For subscription management only

### No Third-Party Analytics or Advertising
We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Security

- All audio files are stored in the app's sandboxed container
- Files are protected by iOS data protection
- No network transmission of audio data
- No cloud backup of recordings (excluded from iCloud)

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

- **Access:** View all your sessions in the app
- **Delete:** Delete individual sessions or all data in Settings
- **Export:** Download a ZIP with your sessions and audio from Settings
- **Opt-out:** Disable all optional analytics and crash reporting

## Children's Privacy

Quiet Coach does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Significant changes will be communicated through app updates.
